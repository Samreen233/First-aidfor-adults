Call for help

If your initial assessment of the situation indicates that the injured person(s) needs medical attention, call for help or ask someone else to call for help. Do not move an injured person unless the situation is life-threatening (e.g. a fire in the car), as you may cause further harm, especially in the case of neck and spinal injuries. If the situation is life-threatening and the person needs to be moved urgently, do so as a last resort without endangering yourself and those around you.
