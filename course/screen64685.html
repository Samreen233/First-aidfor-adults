Treating minor burns

It is important that you do not cool the burn with very cold water or ice, as the extreme cold could cause more damage.

Cool the burn

Cooling should always be the immediate course of action for a minor burn. Of course, you may not always have access to cool running water which would allow the burn to be cooled for a minimum of ten minutes. If you only have a small amount of water in a bottle, for example, you could cover the burn in a clean wet cloth, or if there is no water immediately at hand, another cool clean fluid could be used, such as a soft drink. In this situation, you should always look for cool water immediately, but you may need to be a little more creative until water becomes available.
