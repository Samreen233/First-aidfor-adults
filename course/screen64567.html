Key principles of first aid

These principles will help you build your first aid knowledge and skills. Study them carefully, as they are the foundation of everything that you will learn in the topics to follow. 

Stay Calm

Do NOT take risks that could cause injury to yourself, the injured person, or any bystanders.

Assess the situation

Make sure that it is safe for you and those around you. A good first aider is someone who is aware of their surroundings and ready to act quickly if required.

Manage the person

In addition to providing first aid, managing the person can include keeping them warm or comforting and reassuring them.

Do things step by step

Applying what you know in a step-wise manner will help you stay calm and provide effective first aid in an emergency.
