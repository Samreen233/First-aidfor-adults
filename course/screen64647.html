Imagine that it's a weekend and you've decided to take a walk around the lake near your house. Halfway through your walk, you stop to rest and begin talking to an elderly woman. She tells you she had a stroke two years ago.

From the options below, what answer best describes a stroke?

✅ Correct! A stroke happens when blood supply to part of the brain is interrupted.
❌ Incorrect. A stroke occurs when the blood supply to part of the brain is cut off.
