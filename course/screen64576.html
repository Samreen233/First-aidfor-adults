How has the course been adapted?

The information included in the adapted lessons are specifically tailored to the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. The adaptations are intended to limit the first aiderâ€™s proximity to the casualty, so as to reduce the risk of spreading the virus. What you learn in these lessons should be used to compliment what you learn in the general first aid lessons. Please remember that immediately after providing first aid, you must monitor yourself for symptoms over the next 14 days, especially if you have provided CPR or placed a casualty in the recovery position.  

Emergencies are always stressful, and it is normal that during this pandemic you may be more stressed, experience sadness, or fear. Make sure that you reach out and the seek support if needed. You can speak to your loved ones, contact your local healthcare provider, or contact your local Red Cross Red Crescent National Society to find out what kind of support they can provide you during this time. Also, look out for others who may also be in need - remember, physically distanced but socially close!
