Scenario 2

The person shows signs of complete airway obstruction but is still responsive. In this case, there is no time to waste:

  • Give five targeted back blows to try and dislodge the obstruction
  • Call for help or shout for someone else to get help whilst simultaneously providing back blows
  • If five back blows fail to relieve the obstruction, move on to giving up to five abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver)
