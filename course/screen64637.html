COVID-19 and choking

If you are going to deliver first aid to someone who is choking in the context of COVID, start by assessing the casualty by asking the person to speak. If the casualty is able to speak or is coughing forcefully, encourages the casualty to keep coughing. If the casualty is unable to speak or is coughing weakly:

  • Ask someone to call emergency services
  • Thoroughly but quickly wash your hands with soap and water (or an alcohol-based hand gel or ash if running water is unavailable)
  • Wear your mask
  • Perfom the same steps to support someone who is choking, as learnt earlier in Module 3
