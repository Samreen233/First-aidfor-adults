Key points on psychological first aid

Psychological first aid is a very important skill and one that many of you will already possess. Essentially it involves acting in a way that reassures and calms the injured person, as well as supporting them to seek further help if required.

Psychological first aid is the compassionate and caring support offered to a person or a group of people after they have experienced a very distressing event or situation. It is a method of helping that involves assessing needs and providing emotional support and practical help.
