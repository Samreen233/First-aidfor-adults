Preventing everyday accidents

Learning first aid is not only about the practical techniques and skills. All good first aiders also possess the ability to anticipate potential dangers and take action to avoid or minimize the chance of someone getting injured. In many ways, first aid is actually a last resort - only required when something has gone wrong or an accident has happened. Of course, accidents happen, but those risks can be minimized by modifying behaviors and taking active steps to prevent the likelihood of an accident occurring.
