You're at a friend's house for their birthday dinner where people are dancing and enjoying themselves. Your friend is snacking on some nuts when you notice them starting to cough. They seem to be having trouble breathing.

What do you do?

✅ Correct! If a person is coughing and able to breathe, you should encourage coughing and closely monitor them.
❌ Incorrect. If the person is coughing effectively, you should watch them closely and not intervene yet.
