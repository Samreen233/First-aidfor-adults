Unresponsive person in the car

We learnt how to assess and provide first aid to someone who is unresponsive in an earlier module, but what if someone is unresponsive in a car? The principles are the same - we need to check to see if they are breathing normally and ensure that their airway remains open if possible. In the case of road accidents, checking for breathing and keeping the airway open are especially important because of the risk of commonly seen by first responders in road traffic accidents.
