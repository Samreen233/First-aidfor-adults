How to wear a mask

The following signs and symptoms may indicate that a person is infected with COVID-19:

  • a cough;
  • difficulty breathing;
  • fever or feeling of fever (chills, hot and cold)
  • headaches, aches, sweats and intense fatigue
  • diarrhoea;
  • loss of smell (without nasal obstruction), total loss of taste.

If the casualty has a cough, fever, chills, contact a medical assistant or medical support organisation. If the victim has shortness of breath while resting or exerting themselves, or any other sign that may suggest a life-threatening emergency, call the emergency services or take the person to hospital
