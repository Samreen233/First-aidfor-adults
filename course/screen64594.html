The recovery position and COVID-19

If you determine that the person is unresponsive but still breathing normally you should:

  • Lay the casualty on their back
  • Do not tilt the casualty’s head backwards to clear the airway
  • Do not lean over the casualty’s face, do not put your ear and cheek over the casualty’s mouth and nose
  • Verify that the casualty is breathing by checking if their stomach and chest are rising approaching their mouth.
  • Alert emergency services and follow their instructions.
  • Put the casualty in the recovery position if advised by by the emergency services. If emergency services can arrive quickly, they might tell you to leave the casualty lying on their back in order to avoid any close contact.
