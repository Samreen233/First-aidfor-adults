Question

What are the three basic steps of psychological first aid?

Look for those that need help and determine who to prioritize.
Listen actively to the person, show that you accept their feelings and be supportive and respectful to help calm them.
Lie and tell them whatever you think they need to hear to help calm them down.
Leave the area and distract them from the situation.
Link them to further help or to those that can support them like family and friends. Try to help them with their basic needs in that moment.
