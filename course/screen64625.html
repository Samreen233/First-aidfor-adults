How to give back blows

Step 1

Support the front of the person's chest with one hand and lean them forward so that when the object is dislodged, it comes out of the mouth and does not travel further down the airway

Step 2

Give five sharp blows between the person's shoulder blades, using the heel of your hand

Step 3

Check to see if each back blow has loosened or removed the obstruction. The aim is to give back blows only until the obstruction is loosened or removed. Once the airway is cleared, you do not need to continue to give back blows

Step 4

If back blows fail, move on to the abdominal thrusts (Heimlich Maneuver)
