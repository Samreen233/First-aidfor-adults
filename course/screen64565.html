COVID-19

This course has been adapted to teach critical first aid considerations in the context of an infectious disease outbreak, such as COVID-19. Some modules will include an additional lesson covering the steps that you should follow to keep yourself and others safe when providing first aid in a pandemic context. Remember, there may be variation in international standards for first aid as more data becomes available and as our knowledge about COVID-19 increases. Therefore, it is important that you keep up to date with the evolution of the pandemic in your country through your public authorities and local media.
