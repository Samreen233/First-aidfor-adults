Key points on psychological first aid

Psychological first aid is:

  • Comforting people and helping them to feel calm
  • Providing practical care and support
  • Helping people to address immediate basic needs such as providing water, information, or simply a blanket, for example
  • Listening to people, but not pressuring them to talk
  • Helping people connect to information, services and social support

In order to provide effective psychological first aid, use the action principles of Look, Listen, and Link.
