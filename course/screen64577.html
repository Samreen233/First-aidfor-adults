General protection measures when providing first aid in the context of COVID-19

In the context of an infectious disease outbreak, you will continue to provide first aid as usual - the fundamental principles of first aid that we covered are still applicable. However, there are some additional prevention measures to lower the risk of disease transmission. The measures include:

  • Washing your hands regularly, both before AND after providing first aid
  • Practicing physical distancing by staying at least 1 meter away from other people. When providing first aid, you are often required to be close to other people, so if physical distancing is not possible, you should wear a mask. Learn how to use a mask correctly in the image below
  • Staying at home if you are sick
