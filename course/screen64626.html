How to give abdominal thrusts
  1. Keep the person leaning forward. This will keep the object from falling back down the airway once dislodged
  2. Stand behind the choking person, clench one hand into a fist and place your fist on the middle of their abdomen, just below their ribcage (as shown in the photo). Place your other hand over the fist
  3. Pull inwards and upwards into their chest. Do this a total of five times or until the obstruction is dislodged
  4. If the obstruction is still in place after five back blows and five abdominal thrusts, continue alternating between five back blows and five abdominal thrusts until help arrives
