Imagine that you are shopping with a friend. It is nearing the end of the day and you are ready to go home when all of a sudden, you hear a lady shouting for help. You rush over and find her tending to a man who is lying on the floor with his eyes closed. He is unresponsive without any noticeable injuries.

What do you do?
Begin chest compressions immediately and instruct the lady to get medical help
Give rescue breaths (mouth-to-mouth resuscitation) immediately and instruct the lady to get medical help
Open the man's airway, look and feel for signs of breathing and instruct the lady to get medical help
Cover the man with a blanket to keep him warm and instruct the lady to get medical help
✅ Correct. Checking airway and breathing is the immediate priority.
❌ Incorrect. First assess airway and breathing, then seek medical help.
